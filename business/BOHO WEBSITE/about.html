Boho Kind CO.

made with love daily. based out of orlando florida, Boho Kind came alive with an idea from our creator heather. heather fell in love with the look of tapestries every time she came across one in a store, but she could never get herself to spend the large amount of money that came with them. that is when our brand came alive, heather wanted to create reasonably priced wall hangings so everyone could add a tapestry to their home.
